Get to know the Rules

We know it’s hard to cope up with rules but they are essential to maintain a fair challenge and you’ll have to face some in real life too, So why not now.

  • Only architecture students & professionals can participate

  • The design should belong to you & should be original

  • Only one entry per person is allowed

  • Strictly follow the minimum requirements & basic design guidelines

  • Oh! Lastly be bold about your design

We believe Design creates culture. Culture shapes values. Values determine the future.
